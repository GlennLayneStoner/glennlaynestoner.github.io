In one page

Coherence: undifferentiated information potential. Not a field in space — a pre-structured unity of possible outcomes.

Decoherence: resolution into record. Measurement is the act that converts potential into outcomes.

Cost: resolving information produces heat. At minimum, kBT ln 2 per unbiased bit — the Landauer bound.

Claim: structure — energy, spacetime, matter — emerges as coherence resolves. The universe keeps score in entropy.
